The Sights Of New York

New York is one of the biggest cities in the world, so it's naturally going to have some beautiful sights.

Hopefully, after this page, you'll not only get a sense of what New York feels like but where you might want to go.

This first collection of photo belongs to, you guessed it, Time Sq. Known for its neon lights and buildings, the spot never fails to attract attention year round.

While a neon New York is great, it's still important to spend as much time as possible in nature, especially in an industrial city like New York. Central park is a great place to relax, but there are some hidden gems out such as sakura park and prospect park.
